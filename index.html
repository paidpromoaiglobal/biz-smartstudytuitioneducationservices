About Us

Smart study tuition education services

Smart Study Tuition Education Services is a trusted and highly-rated tuition centre located in Udhaya Nagar, Porur, Chennai. With a perfect 5-star rating from our happy families, we are committed to delivering quality education to students right from kindergarten through higher classes. Our experienced and friendly staff work closely with each student to ensure they understand concepts clearly and perform their very best in academics.

Situated near PJN Mahal on Udhaya Nagar Main Road, our centre provides a safe and welcoming atmosphere for all children, including girls. We are open six days a week from early morning to evening, making it convenient for families with varying schedules. At Smart Study, we don't just teach — we guide, inspire, and help every child build a strong foundation for a bright future.
